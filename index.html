Introduction by
Gavin Canaan
Education Programs Manager
We have Big News! We are excited to announce a webinar interview with legendary FOH engineer Big Mick Hughes as part of Meyer Sound's Mixing Workshop webinar series. Two webinar sessions are scheduled each day for July 1st and 2nd. Big Mick is a giant of the world of FOH engineers. Don't miss these amazing opportunities to join the live interviews and Q&A with Big Mick Hughes!.

In other news, the InfoComm tradeshow takes place this week in Orlando and Meyer Sound will have numerous activities going on at the tradeshow. Demos of the new LEOPARD system will be conducted at the Valencia Ballroom during the show and public listening sessions take place Wednesday and Thursday, June 17–18 at 15:00, 16:00, and 17:00, and on Friday, June 19 at 15:00 and 16:00.

Additional seminars and education activities are always in the works, so stay in touch and visit the Education page of the Meyer Sound website for additional details and updates.
